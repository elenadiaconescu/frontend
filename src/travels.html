Travels

Since I was a kid I want to travel around the world, I felt my hometown, Bacau, was to small for me and I can do more.
When I was 15 years old I join a colleague organization and I start organizing events and travel trough this, all around Romania.
By the time I moved out from my hometown, I visited all the main cities and I was ready to take it to the next level.

In Bucharest, I joined several organizations but my main focus was EESTEC, that help me out travel all around Europe.
Once I had a good image of each culture, and after finishing university I decided to move to Portugal.
I visited the beautiful land from south to north, east to west. My university was in Lisbon so I made this city my home for 4 years.
